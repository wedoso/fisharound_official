Support

Fish Around is an offline pond game for iPhone and iPad.

Email support
How do I restore progress?

Progress is stored only on your device and currently does not sync through an account or iCloud. Deleting the app removes local progress.

Why can’t I save a pond photo?

Open iOS Settings, find Fish Around, and allow it to add photos. The app requests add-only access and cannot browse your existing library.

How do I reset the pond?

Open Settings inside the app and choose “Forget memory.” Language and sound preferences are preserved.

Does Fish Around require the internet?

No. The current release works offline and does not include accounts, advertising, analytics, or online purchases.

How do pond reminders work?

Pond reminders are on by default after iOS notification permission is granted. Local reminders may appear after about 22 hours, 3 days, and 7 days away. Opening Fish Around resets the reminder timer for the next time you leave. You can turn them off in the app or in iOS Settings.

How do I report a bug?

Email us with your device model, iOS version, app version, what you expected, and what happened. A screenshot is helpful when available.
